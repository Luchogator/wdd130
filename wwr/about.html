Welcome to Rafting Adventures

We are committed to providing the best whitewater rafting experience! Join us on an unforgettable adventure filled with excitement and breathtaking landscapes. Our team of experienced guides will ensure your safety and enjoyment every step of the way.

History

Founded in 1995, Rafting Adventures is one of the leading rafting companies in Peru. Located in the heart of the Amazon rainforest, we offer a unique and thrilling experience navigating some of the most beautiful and challenging rivers in the region. Over the past decades, we have welcomed thousands of adventurers from all over the world.

Looking beyond our borders, we are also working on expanding internationally. With increasing interest in adventure tourism, we plan to open centers in neighboring countries like Ecuador and Bolivia, and eventually in other international destinations. Our goal is to make Rafting Adventures a global brand known for providing safe, exhilarating, and unforgettable rafting experiences.

As part of our commitment to bringing the excitement of rafting to more people, we are proud to announce an ambitious expansion plan. By 2025, Rafting Adventures aims to open new rafting centers across the country, starting in regions like Cusco, Arequipa, and the northern highlands. These new centers will offer both beginner and advanced rafting courses, as well as team-building activities for businesses.

At Rafting Adventures, we not only promote rafting as a sport but as a way to connect with nature and promote sustainable tourism. We work closely with local communities to protect the rivers and natural habitats that are essential to the success of our activities. Our guides are trained not just in rafting, but in environmental conservation and the cultural significance of the regions where we operate.
