History

Founded in 1995, Rafting Adventures is one of the leading rafting companies in Peru. Located in the heart of the Amazon rainforest, we offer a unique and thrilling experience navigating some of the most beautiful and challenging rivers in the region.

Our company prides itself on its commitment to safety and excellence. We use state-of-the-art equipment that is maintained to the highest standards, ensuring our clients have a safe and enjoyable experience. We are also known for having one of the most qualified teams of guides and professionals, who speak multiple languages, including English, Spanish, and French.
